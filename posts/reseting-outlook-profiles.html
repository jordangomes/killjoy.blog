how to reset your outlook profile

the simplest way to fix most outlook issues

why?

Resetting your outlook profile can fix many outlook issues. Anthing from Oulook not loading to a plethora of weird bugs.

alright how do I do it?

  1. Close outlook entirely, every window must go

  2. On your keyboard press + R to open the run window

  3. In the Open box type “outlook.exe /profiles” and press Enter or click okay

    you should be presented with a screen similiar to the below

  4. Click Options then click New

  5. You should be presented with this setup screen from here you can proceed to sign in with your outlook account as usual.

  6. Once you have added your account you should see a screen like this. Uncheck “Setup Outlook on my phone, too” and then press Done

  7. Finally back at the Choose Profile screen make sure the new profile we created is selected, check Set as default profile and click OK

  8. That’s it your outlook profile is reset. Give it a bit to load.
